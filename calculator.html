Limitation
Calculator

Pick a case type, enter the relevant date, and get your filing deadline under the First Schedule of the Limitation Act, 1908. Quick reference for Pakistani practitioners — not a replacement for checking the statute yourself.

Before you rely on this: Deadlines move. Court orders, condonation applications, and local registry practice can all shift the date you get here. Verify with a qualified practitioner before acting on any result.

Section 12 lets you exclude the time you spent getting certified copies of the judgment or decree. If that doesn't apply to your matter, leave this at 0.
