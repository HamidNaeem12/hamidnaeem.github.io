AI Governance & Regulatory Analysis

Organizations deploying AI rarely know where they actually stand on compliance — because the frameworks are genuinely complex and still moving. I map systems against the EU AI Act, US executive orders, and emerging South Asian regulatory developments, and produce analysis that is actionable without requiring a law degree to read.

Regulatory Gap Analysis Policy Drafting Compliance Frameworks EU AI Act FIA / PDMA
02

Legal Research & Litigation Support

I draft for actual cases — petitions, legal notices, plaints, sale deeds — across Pakistan and India. Not practice problems. Documents filed with clients' names on them before real courts. That context changes the standard of care applied to every sentence.

Plaints & Petitions Legal Notices Case Law Research Sale & Rent Deeds Family Law
03

Data Analysis & Legal Intelligence

Courts produce data at scale. Almost no one reads it systematically. I analyze case outcomes, enforcement patterns, and regulatory filing trends — and convert findings into dashboards and reports that inform real decisions, not slide decoration.

Python Dataset Analysis Dashboard Design Trend Reports Excel · Looker Studio

Legal Writing & Policy Communication
