Writing & Resources

Not a blog.
A toolkit.

When I started, there was nothing. No guide, no senior to ask, no roadmap for someone trying to sit at the intersection of law, data, and AI in Pakistan. I failed at things. I figured things out the hard way. I'm still learning — I won't pretend otherwise. But I built these pages so that wherever you are in this journey, you don't have to feel as lost as I once did. That's the only reason any of this exists.

The hard way teaches you things no guide ever could. But you still deserve a guide.
— What I wish someone had told me at the start
