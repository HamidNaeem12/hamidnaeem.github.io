Writing  /  Resource Library

The Library

Tools, databases, and references I actually use. Organized by what they help you do, not by category name. No sponsored placement — something has to earn its spot here.

AI Governance & Regulation Primary texts and monitoring tools
Primary Law

EU AI Act — Official Text

The consolidated regulation directly from EUR-Lex. Read the annexes — they define what "high-risk" actually means in practice, not the summaries.

 Framework

NIST AI RMF

The US risk management framework. More flexible than EU law but more practically useful for building compliance arguments across jurisdictions.

 Policy Monitor

OECD AI Policy Observatory

Tracks AI policies across 70+ countries including Pakistan, India, and UAE. Useful for cross-jurisdictional comparison without reading every national document.

 Annual Report

Stanford HAI — AI Index

The most credible annual survey of AI research, policy, and adoption trends. The policy chapter is the one worth reading carefully.
Legal Research Databases and court resources
Pakistan

Pakistan Law Site

Searchable database of Pakistani legislation and superior court judgments. The IHC, LHC, and Supreme Court decisions are reasonably up to date.

 India

Indian Kanoon

Free full-text search across Indian court judgments from all High Courts and the Supreme Court. Underused by non-Indian researchers despite its quality.

 International

ICANN / Internet Governance

For matters touching digital identity, domain disputes, and internet governance law — jurisdictionally complex but increasingly relevant.

 Academic

Google Scholar — Case Law

Underrated for finding legal scholarship. The "Cited by" function is useful for tracing how a judgment has been interpreted across subsequent cases.
Data Privacy Regulators, guidance, and frameworks
GDPR

EDPB — European Data Protection Board

Official guidance on GDPR interpretation. The opinions and guidelines are more useful than most academic commentary on GDPR compliance questions.

 US / CCPA

California AG — CCPA Resources

Official CCPA guidance and enforcement actions. The enforcement letters are the most informative documents for understanding what the AG prioritizes.

 Global Tracker

IAPP Global Privacy Law Tracker

Tracks the status of privacy legislation across all major jurisdictions. The Pakistan PDPA status is included — worth checking quarterly.

 Pakistan

Pakistan — Personal Data Protection Bill

Track legislative progress and any draft regulations from the regulator. Still evolving — check for updates before relying on older summaries.
Newsletters Worth Reading Signal over noise
My Own

The Digital Gavel

My newsletter covering AI governance, digital rights, and international law with a South Asian lens. Published when there is something worth saying.

 Critical AI

AI Snake Oil — Narayanan & Kapoor

The most rigorous critical perspective on AI claims and hype. Essential reading before citing any AI capability study in a legal or policy argument.

 Policy

Lawfare

Serious analysis at the intersection of law and national security, including digital governance. The AI and tech law coverage is consistently high-quality.

 Global South

Tech Policy Press

Good coverage of tech policy issues with genuine attention to non-Western jurisdictions. Fills a gap that most US-centric newsletters ignore entirely.
